THE SPACE

Built for Two

Each of the four cottages is completely separate — its own entrance, its own veranda, its own view of the lake. No shared walls, no shared paths.

Polished concrete floors, a handmade teak bed, velvet armchairs, sliding glass doors that open straight to the outside. The bathroom has no roof — you shower under the trees and look up at the palms.

It's not a hotel. It's better made than most.
